About

Rrural Capital Unu holds, through its 100% owned subsidiary Rural Capital Unu, a wind farm project under development in Romania. The project is based on 344 hectares of land in the municipality of Braila, which have been fully purchased for the project. The permitting of the land for the purpose of a 100+MW windfarm are well advanced, and negotiations with developer/constructors ongoing with a view to bring the project to completion as a working generating facility.
